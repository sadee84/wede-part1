HomeKnitt

founded in 2023 by Sade Mzila

HomeKnitt is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing warmth and comfort to those in need. Our volunteers knit and crochet blankets, hats, and scarves for the homeless, elderly, and others who are struggling.

Our Mission

Our mission is to spread warmth and kindness one stitch at a time. We believe that everyone deserves to feel loved and cared for, and we're committed to making a difference in our community.
